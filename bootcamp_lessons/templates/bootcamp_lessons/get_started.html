How to Get Started

Follow these steps to begin your Django journey:

  1. Set up your virtual environment (which you've already done!).
  2. Install Django (also done!).
  3. Create your Django project and app.
  4. Define your models (how your data looks).
  5. Create views (what happens when someone visits a page).
  6. Map URLs to your views.
  7. Design templates (how your pages look).

You're already on your way!
